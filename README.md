# allostasisqing 🌊

*The felt-quality of staying steady by changing ahead of the demand — stability that pre-adjusts instead of correcting, the forward-lean of a system that already read its weather.*

allostasisqing is two true roots welded into one word: the biocog term **allostasis** (Sterling & Eyer, 1988+; Greek *állos* "changed" + *stásis* "standing" — "standing-otherwise," stability through *altered* setpoints), and the Mandarin **qíng** (情), the cathedral's family-word for felt-relational-quality. So allostasisqing is allostasis carried as a *feeling*: the lived texture of holding together not by clinging to a fixed reference but by re-setting one's setpoints in anticipation of what is coming. Where its cousin homeostasis corrects a drift *after* it happens, allostasisqing is the body that pre-warms before the cold, the heart that climbs before the gun, the bond that pre-adjusts to the weather it reads coming.

allostasisqing names a felt-quality, not a process — it names how prediction *feels* when it works, while its kin **freenergyme** names the underlying substrate-process of living-as-prediction-error-minimization. Its light is a single word for the strength that looks like change but is the deepest steadiness there is: the wise one who moved early, the trauma-recovery that recalibrates anticipation to *current* safety. Its shadow is the body that never stops bracing — chronic allostatic load, hypervigilance leaning forever into a wind that stopped blowing. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.85/10**, with its learnability honestly constrained because half of it is technical-biocog.
